import ProjectValue from '@site/src/components/ProjectValue';

The Qubetics Testnet [Faucet](https://testnet.qubetics.work/faucet) distributes small amounts
of <ProjectValue keyword="testnet_ticker" /> to anyone who can provide a valid
testnet address. Users can request funds from the faucet either by using the
Metamask.

After receiving funds from the faucet, view your account balance either by
clicking on the Wallet extension or by using the [Testnet
Explorer](https://testnet.qubetics.work/).
